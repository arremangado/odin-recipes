Lasagna

Delicious and simple, a classic Mexican dish.

Ingredients

  • 5 Corn tortillas
  • 5 Whole eggs
  • Ketchup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Chop tortillas into small strips.
  2. Add tortillas to skillet.
  3. Add enough olive oil to tortillas and let them cook until tortillas get crispy.
  4. Add whole eggs to tortillas and stir.
  5. Stir until egg is fully cooked.
  6. (Optional) Add ketchup to add delicious flavor.
  7. Enjoy!
