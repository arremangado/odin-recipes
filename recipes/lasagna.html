Lasagna

Delicious and simple, a classic Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Monterey Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • 80/20 Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sheets

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to a temperature of 450F.
  2. Grab an appropriate oven container.
  3. Line the container with foil sheets.
  4. Spray the container with olive oil.
  5. Fully pad the container with pasta sheets.
  6. Add ground meat and monterey cheese to cotainer and thoroughly spread out.
  7. Repeat steps 5 & 6 until you're happy with the results.
  8. Put lasagna in the oven for 30 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
